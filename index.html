It's Nice to Meet You!

Hi, my name is Valerie Remaker and I am a junior double majoring in Human Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE) and Psychology at the University of Washington. I really love this combination of fields of study, becuase as designers and engineers, we design for human needs and desires, rather than designing for optimized machines and or algorithms. A major in psychology focuses on understanding human behavior which I can apply in the design process. I am very passionate about K-12 Engineering outreach to help get the future generations of engineers interested in Engineering. I am currently an Engineering Ambassador for The College Of Engineering at The University of Washington!
I plan to use my degree to pursue user experience design and web development! I also have an interst in designing and developing classroom technology.

Crochet Buddy

In the Spring of 2020, around the start of the Novel Coronavirus Pandemic that caused countries to shut down, I took up the art of crochet to fill the days in isolation. Due to my inability to count my stitches correctly in each of the rows I completed, the first blanket I made came out as more of a trapezoid than a rectangle. The other big issue that most novice crochet hobbyists is keeping a consistent tension in the amount that you pull on the yarn to create each stitch. Crochet Buddy is an Internet of Things (IoT) device created to assist novice crochet hobbyists in their pursuit of beautiful yarn-craft.
